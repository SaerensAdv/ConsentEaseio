# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing pages on `consentease.io`
- Public product and feature pages
- Public comparison, solutions, compliance, docs, blog, scan, roadmap, brand, and contact/help pages
- Public agency profile pages (`/agency/:slug`)

## Out of scope
- Authenticated dashboard routes (`/dashboard/**`)
- Authentication/account flows (`/login`, `/onboarding`, `/forgot-password`, `/reset-password`, `/verify-email*`, `/account/**`)
- Internal API endpoints except where they directly affect crawlability or indexation assets such as `robots.txt` or `sitemap.xml`

## Target audience
- Small businesses that need GDPR/CCPA cookie consent compliance
- Agencies managing consent banners for client websites

## Primary keywords
- cookie consent banner
- GDPR cookie consent
- CCPA cookie consent
- consent management platform
- Google Consent Mode v2
- cookie scanner
